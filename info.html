Privacy policy

Updated: 26/Feb/2013

By visiting or using any of the features of Sentimentron ("the product") you consent to it storing some information you provide. This document sets out what that information is and how its used. In short:

  • Sentimentron records the queries you enter to provide results.
  • If you choose, Sentimentron stores an email address you've provided in order to alert you when a query finishes. This information is deleted after the query has been completed.
  • Amazon Web Services, Inc. has been authorized to collect information about when results are accessed. These log files are retained for 90 days.
  • Sentimentron's API servers collect log information to prevent misuse. These log files are retained for 90 days.
  • Sentimentron doesn't explicity share any information with others.

Recording your queries

When you enter a query into Sentimentron, it's indefinitely stored. This allows Sentimentron to offer permanent links to results, and analyse what the site's used for to improve future results. No information is stored alongside the query which can personally identify you.

Your email address

If queries take a long time to process. Sentimentron offers to store your email address. This allows Sentimentron to optionally contact you when the query is complete and offer a link to the results. Once you've been contacted, Sentimentron deletes your email address. Sentimentron does not share your email address with third-parties.

Sentimentron uses Google Apps to provide email services. If you send email to any sentimentron.co.uk address (e.g. to submit a bug report or feedback) then your message will be stored in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Technical information about the machine you're using

When you submit a query, Sentimentron records the physical address of your Internet connection, the time you made the the request, your Browser's user-agent string and the URL you requested. This URL includes information about the query being processed and may be visible to 3rd-parties unaffiliated with Sentimentron. Sentimentron collects this information to prevent abuse.

When you retrieve the results of a query, Amazon Webservices Inc. records the time you retrieved the results, the identifier of which query produced them, your browser's user agent string and the page you were referred from. Sentimentron collects this information to monitor resource consumption.

Information sharing policy

Sentimentron doesn't share any of your information with 3rd-parties, and takes reasonable technical measures to protect your personal information. Communication with some parts of Sentimentron are not secured.
