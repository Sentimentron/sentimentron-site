Examples

To use any of these examples, copy them into the search box.

View results from a specific site 

arstechnica.com

View results from pages with a keyword 

iPhone

Sentimentron will automatically expand your query to include similar terms. If you don't want them, use + to prevent expansion.

+iPhone

You can also use - to exclude certain expansions.

iPod -"iPod Touch"

View results from sites matching a keyword

You can use & to combine results. 

iPhone & daringfireball.net

Combine queries

You can combine multiple queries using & and/or |

 (iPhone | iPod) & arstechnica.com 
 ALCOA | AXP
